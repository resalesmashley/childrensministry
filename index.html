About BCC Children's Ministry

The Children's Ministry at Bengal Christian Church is dedicated to partnering with parents to help children develop a genuine, life-long relationship with Jesus Christ.

Our Mission

To create a safe, nurturing environment where children can:

  • Learn about God's love through engaging, age-appropriate lessons
  • Experience authentic worship and prayer
  • Build meaningful relationships with peers and mentors
  • Develop character and biblical values
  • Discover and use their God-given gifts and talents

Our Vision

We envision a generation of children who know Jesus personally, understand the Bible, and live out their faith with confidence and joy in every area of their lives.

Core Values

  • Bible-Centered: God's Word is the foundation of everything we teach
  • Grace-Filled: We extend love, acceptance, and forgiveness to all children
  • Fun & Engaging: Learning about Jesus should be exciting and memorable
  • Safe & Secure: We maintain the highest standards of child safety and protection
  • Family Partnership: We support and equip parents as the primary spiritual leaders

What We Teach
